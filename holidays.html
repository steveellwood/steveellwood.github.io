Key Holidays undertaken

Foreign

I suppose, largely, foreign is a matter of perspective. From the point of view of two English people, living in Scotland, pretty much anything off mainland UK is what counts. So that's what we'll call them.

New Zealand was probably our biggest and most impressive holiday. Still by all accounts as wonderful as it was when we visited, and the only country I've ever wanted to emigrate to.

Singapore was almost a whim visit. The M.E.N., or Manchester Evening news had advertised a holiday "Christmas at Raffles", and I thought it sounded expensive. We decided to price it up, and found we could get a much better price as solo travellers. So we did.

Spain records a visit with Steve's kids to their grandfather who retired to Spain many years ago.
