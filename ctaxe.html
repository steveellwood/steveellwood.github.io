Steve's borrowed knife

Sorry to disappoint. A Camp Tramp is a *big knife*

Knowing of my interest in Swamp Rat knives, following my order of a Rat Trap, and growing interest in their FB range, a rash but characteristically generous mojo decided to lend me a Camp Tramp for a look-see.

This is a real imaginary pirates knife. A true Crocodile Dundee "Nah, that's not a knife. *THIS* is a knife" knife.

It's big. Black with a crinkle coating on it. It's made out of 1/4" steel F"goodness"sake! The handle is a proprietary Resiprene, comfortably firm but yielding in the handle suitable for long work with the blade. These blades have a "certain reputation" not harmed by some of the daft things that the *makers* state as reproducible. Chopping concrete blocks? Bending to 45 degrees in a vice?

Stay calm stuarty, I didn't do that. I chopped some vegetables, and stuck it into some wood. SWMBO quite likes the knife. What she'll think when the BG (2" longer) arrives...

I'd like to give you some naff impressions of what the knife is like.

I'll set it in context.

Nah.

This is a big knife.

The knife is huge in the hand.

The characteristic finger choil after the handle does permit more delicate work. Who am I kidding? This knife cuts slices off Abrams tanks...

Thanks to mojo for the loan, and apologies for the naff photos and tale!
