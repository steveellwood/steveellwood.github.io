Cromarty

Cromarty is a small town at the end of the Black Isle

Its motto is Meane Weil, Speak Weil, and Doe Weil.

It has long history and has repeatedly gone through cycles of depression and growth. You'll learn more about Cromarty as we go on.

The town has its own website.
