The Ellwood Family

Site Purpose

This site is about the life and activities of the Ellwood family of Cromarty.

A large part of this is Steve's desire to update his 10 year old knowledge of how websites work. He's not done anything significant since ooh, about 1995. Since he is editor of the local website, he thought it might be sensible to understand how HTMl worked nowadays. Much to his horror, he now finds things called style sheets and XHTML. He is very pleased that the entire site is currently handcoded using PFE, but admits to using Nvu for some grunt work. It doesn't do good XHTML though... and needs tidying. We've only been here for three years but like it a great deal.

We'll provide pictures of the area and the things that interest us. There will be links to Holiday information, both about where we've been , and where we recommend.

Recommendations

We travel round with three dogs, see below. For most places that makes you very unwelcome.

In Cromarty, you get a friendly welcome and real ale at the Cromarty Arms, but that's cheating a bit, as we live in Cromarty...

On the West side of Scotland, in Gairloch, you get nice rooms, good food and real ale at the Old Inn. Very busy in the summer, but well worth a visit at anytime.

On Skye, a really nice welcoming hotel is the Uig Hotel - and even their own duck eggs for breakfast, if you're lucky.

Background
